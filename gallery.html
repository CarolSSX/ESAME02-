IRIS VAN HERPEN
ARTICLE
//2021

//Communication
//Graphic Design
//Copywriting

The article explores the groundbreaking HEARTHRISE collection by Iris Van Herpen, a fusion of fashion, technology, and nature. This presentation highlights how Van Herpen continues to push the boundaries of haute couture by incorporating innovative materials and techniques such as 3D printing and laser cutting.The HEARTHRISE collection draws inspiration from organic forms and natural phenomena, mimicking the growth patterns of crystals and the fluidity of water. The designs embody a delicate balance between strength and fragility, showcasing Van Herpen’s vision for the future of fashion as a bridge between art and science.
